Privacy policy

Privacy should match the product story.

Kronroe the product is designed to keep memory local. Kronroe.dev is simpler: the website can use optional analytics if you allow them, and release emails are opt-in. We do not sell personal data.

Open the homepage controls Commercial licensing

What stays local

Kronroe’s in-browser playground runs the graph engine in WebAssembly. Facts you enter there stay in the browser session unless you explicitly export them.

What the site may collect

If you allow analytics on the homepage, Kronroe.dev can record page views and basic device/browser information through Google Analytics.

What release emails use

If you submit the release form, we store the email address you provide so we can send release announcements. Nothing else in that form is required.

Optional analytics

Kronroe.dev keeps analytics off until you opt in through the homepage privacy controls. If you allow them, Google Analytics may process page URLs, referrers, approximate device/browser information, and similar traffic metrics so we can understand how the site is used.

Cookies and browser storage

If analytics are enabled, Google Analytics may use cookies or similar browser identifiers. Separately, the homepage stores your analytics preference locally in your browser so the site remembers your choice.

Release notifications

The release signup form is used only for release announcements. It is not a general marketing newsletter. We do not sell or trade that email list.

Commercial enquiries

If you contact Kronroe about commercial licensing, we use the details you send to reply and handle that enquiry. Those messages are not shared for advertising.

Your controls

  • You can allow or decline site analytics from the homepage privacy controls.
  • You can ignore the release form and browse the site without signing up.
  • You can contact hi@kronroe.dev if you want your release-notification email removed.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not turn the homepage playground into a cloud service.
  • We do not require an account just to evaluate Kronroe.

Questions?

If something in this policy feels unclear, email hi@kronroe.dev. We would rather explain the tradeoffs plainly than hide them behind legal fog.
